About DUMP-IT

Local hauling for hard jobs and cleaner outcomes.

DUMP-IT is a locally owned construction debris and dump truck hauling company built by hardworking people who understand that debris control matters on real jobsites. We help demolition crews, builders, contractors, property managers, and commercial clients move material out without dragging the schedule down.

Our approach is simple: arrive prepared, work safely, haul efficiently, and route material responsibly whenever possible. Less landfill when practical, fewer delays when it counts, and no nonsense.
